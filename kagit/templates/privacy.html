Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect
  • Account Info: Email, username, and profile picture.
  • Voice Data: Audio recordings from Sound Practice (processed to provide AI feedback).
  • Usage Data: Chat logs with Talk-Bots, practice history, and favorite artists.
2. How We Use Your Information
  • To provide and improve our AI-powered language tools.
  • To personalize your "Agit" experience based on your favorite K-content.
  • To analyze app performance and fix bugs.
3. Data Storage and Security
We implement industry-standard security measures. Your voice data is used primarily for real-time analysis and is not shared with third parties for marketing.
4. Third-Party Services
We may use third-party tools (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase) to monitor app usage. These services have their own privacy policies.
5. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)
Users in certain regions have the right to access, delete, or limit the processing of their data. Contact us at support@k-agit.com to exercise these rights.
6. Children's Privacy
K-AGIT is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
